Minecraft is a game about placing blocks and going on adventures.



Explore randomly-generated worlds and build amazing things from the simplest of homes to the grandest of castles. Play in Creative Mode with unlimited resources or mine deep into the world in survival mode, crafting weapons and armour to fend off the dangerous mobs.




  • Create and explore your very own world where the only limit is what you can imagine

  • Includes five skin packs and two texture packs to customise your gameplay!

  • Use the touch screen to check your map or manage your inventory!




This game is only compatible with New Nintendo 3DS, New Nintendo 3DS XL and New Nintendo 2DS XL.
