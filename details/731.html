Just Dance gives you the opportunity to perform all the iconic dance moves to your favourite songs that you always wanted to perform. Grab the Wiimote and dance to an impressive track list full of famous and unforgettable hits.




  • Follow the moves of a real dancing coach dressed according to the style of the song

  • Your next moves scroll from the right to the left of the screen

  • Learn iconic dance routines

  • Perform the complete choreography and improve your ability to be a good dancer

  • Play with up to 4 friends and invite others to follow your steps

  • Totally immerse yourself in the dance and lose all your inhibitions

  • Around 30 famous tracks available from many different genres such as contemporary 80's, 90's, Disco, rock, pop, r 'n' b

  • Add your own style to the moves to out-do your friends

  • Keep an eye on your score and an ear on your audience

  • Your style and timing are detected and you are rewarded with direct feedback.
