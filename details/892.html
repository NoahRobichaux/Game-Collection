Tetris Worlds places the player in a universe where Tetris is played in different ways on different planets. Players will be able to test their block-dropping skills in the most popular versions of Tetris, or the latest cutting-edge variations of the classic game. Tetris Worlds will also feature multiplayer capabilities allowing Tetris fans to compete directly against one another.




  • Six variations of Tetris gameplay, including the all new Hot-Line Tetris, Cascade Tetris and Fusion Tetris

  • Play Tetris in six different worlds, each evolving as you clear levels

  • Multi-player game lets up to four players see who can clear levels the fastest

  • Simple gameplay, easy to pick up without instruction, and completely addictive
